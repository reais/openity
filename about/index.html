About Us

At Openity, our mission is to deliver solutions that give people the freedom to communicate wherever, whenever, and however they need.

Openity is devoted to developing practical technology solutions and providing unmatched support. We value relationships and view ourselves as partners with our customers and community. We make communicating easy by providing quality, intuitive, and affordable mobile solutions. We strive for worldwide brand recognition built on reliability, support, integrity, and performance.

In addition to One, Openity designs, builds, and manufactures our own custom line of VoIP hardware and software. The Core, Portal, and Bridge unify communications across VoIP, satellite, and cellular.

Openity Core

Core is a modern, easy to use PBX providing VoIP connectivity. Unlike One, Core is a hardware platform allowing customers to host their own PBX on-premise. Core is able to host hundreds of extensions and simultaneous phone calls in a 1U form factor.

Openity Portal

Portal is a GSM to VoIP gateway with seamless integration into Core. With Portal, VoIP calls can be routed across the cellular network at a fraction of the cost of traditional analog phone lines, while also allowing you to take advantage of mobile calling plans.

Openity Bridge

Bridge is a Satellite to VoIP gateway, allowing smartphones and other VoIP devices to place and receive calls via the Globalstar satellite network. With Bridge, customers have communication options in areas not traditionally served by cellular or landline devices.
